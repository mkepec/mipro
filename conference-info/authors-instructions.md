# Instructions for Authors

The MIPRO 2025 "Instructions for Authors" page outlines guidelines for submitting scientific, professional, and review papers for the conference. It specifies that only original papers in English or Croatian are accepted, requiring electronic submission as PDF (and optionally DOC) documents via the MIPRO portal. Authors must adhere strictly to provided Microsoft Word or LaTeX templates, with papers limited to six pages, including illustrations and references. Final submissions must incorporate author details, high-quality figures, and no page numbers, and authors of accepted English papers must complete an IEEE Copyright Form for publication in both conference proceedings and the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library.
