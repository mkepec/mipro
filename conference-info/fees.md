# MIPRO 2026 Conference Fees

The estimated conference fees for MIPRO 2026 are between 150 and 300 Euro.

Based on the MIPRO 2025 conference fees, which may be indicative of 2026, the registration costs were as follows:

*   **Members of MIPRO and IEEE:** 297 EUR for early bird registration and 324 EUR for regular registration.
*   **Students (undergraduate and graduate), primary and secondary school teachers:** 165 EUR for early bird and 180 EUR for regular registration.
*   **Others:** 330 EUR for early bird and 360 EUR for regular registration.

**Note:**

*   The student discount does not apply to PhD students.
*   Authors presenting two or more papers are eligible for a 10% discount.
